The Guerrilla Girls' iconic 1989 advertisement “Do Women Have to be Naked to Get into the Met. Museum?” challenged the historical underrepresentation of female artists and the overrepresentation of female nudes in museum collections. This research project leverages advancements in computer vision to analyze the gender disparity among the nude figures in modern art collections.

View the full data visualization [here](https://fangyizhu.github.io/guerilla-ai/).
